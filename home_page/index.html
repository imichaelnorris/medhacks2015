Your Data in Your Hands!

Our Vision

Our vision is to give our patients control of their own data and allow them to customize who gets to view their data. This will improve data sharing leading to new scientific discoveries, better healthcare, and happier people.

How It Works!

How do we keep the data secure?

How can patients decide who gets to view their data?

Patients can open their portal and select certain fields they want to give access for. and give permissions to various groups that have requested the data.
